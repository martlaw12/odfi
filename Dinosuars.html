My Favorite Dinosaurs

Tyrannosaurus Rex

A large carnivorous dinosaur known for its powerful jaws and tiny arms. Tyrannosaurus rex lived approximately 68 to 66 million years ago during the late Cretaceous period.

Velociraptor

A small, fast, and intelligent dinosaur with sharp claws. Velociraptors lived during the late Cretaceous period and are believed to have hunted in packs.

Stegosaurus

A herbivorous dinosaur known for its distinctive double row of large bony plates along its back and spikes on its tail. Stegosaurus lived during the late Jurassic period.

Brachiosaurus

A gigantic herbivorous dinosaur with a long neck and a large body. Brachiosaurus lived during the Jurassic period and was one of the largest land animals to ever exist.

Triceratops

A herbivorous dinosaur with a frill on its head and three facial horns. Triceratops lived during the late Cretaceous period and was a contemporary of Tyrannosaurus rex.

Apatosaurus

A massive, long-necked herbivorous dinosaur known for its large size. Apatosaurus, formerly known as Brontosaurus, lived during the Jurassic period.
