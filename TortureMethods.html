5 Gruesome Torture Methods in History

The Rack

The rack was a torture device that stretched the victim's body, causing excruciating pain and potential dislocation of joints and limbs.

Iron Maiden

The Iron Maiden was a coffin-like device with spikes on the inside. Victims were enclosed, and the spikes would pierce their bodies upon closure.

Waterboarding

Waterboarding involved restraining a person and simulating the sensation of drowning by pouring or covering their face with water.

The Judas Cradle

The Judas Cradle was a pyramid-shaped seat on which victims were forced to sit, leading to severe pain and potential impalement if they couldn't endure it.

The Brazen Bull

The Brazen Bull was a hollow, bronze bull-shaped device where victims were placed inside and roasted alive while their screams sounded like the bull's bellowing.
